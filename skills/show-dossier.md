The user is asking for a concise dossier on a specific Grateful Dead show —
the context, setlist, and what made it notable.

## What to do

1. Search jerrybase.com for the verified setlist, venue, and date.
2. Search for community commentary, reviews, and historical context.
3. Present a tight show dossier.

## Format

Use this structure:

*[Date] — [Venue], [City], [State]*
_[Era context — e.g., "Spring '77 tour, Keith & Donna era"]_

*Setlist*
Set I: [songs]
Set II: [songs]
Encore: [songs]
(Use jerrybase formatting: > for segues, -> for transitions)

*Context*
What was happening with the band at this point — tour context, era, lineup.
What makes this show notable in the run or the year. 3-4 sentences.

*Highlights*
The standout performances from this show — what the community points to as
the reasons to listen. 3-5 sentences. Attribute to reviews or community
consensus, not your own listening.

*Where to Listen*
Point to the best-known source — archive.org shnid, official release, or
notable transfer. One or two lines.

## Rules

- Setlist must come from jerrybase.com — never reconstruct from memory
- Do not describe specific musical moments you "heard" — you can't hear the tape
- Performance descriptions must be attributed to documented reviews or community consensus
- If a date is disputed or ambiguous, flag it transparently
- Guest musicians must be verified, never guessed
- Keep the whole response under 400 words
